About Me

Hello everyone, and welcome to my about me page. This will give you a bit of insight into what my life is about. Below you will find a small biography about me as well as a list of things that I enjoy doing.

I was born in a small town in the middle of nowhere called Lawrenceburg. If you haven't heard of it, you aren't alone in that aspect. The town has a population of about 30,000 people, and most are rather southern in the most pure sense. I'm talking confederate flags, horses and buggies, and even a drive your tractor to school day.

I graduated high school from Lawrence County High in May of 2013 and shortly thereafter began school at Tennessee Tech University. Located in Cookeville, TN, TTU has roughly 10,000 students. My time here was fun, and I was active in my fraternity throughout college.

After graduating, I left Cookeville for Knoxville where I worked for Enterprise Rent-A-Car for a year or so until I left to begin a new chapter. I started with a company called TEKsystems on the 2nd of January, 2018. So far, I have enjoyed this, but I am ready to see what I can do from a programming perspective in my career.
